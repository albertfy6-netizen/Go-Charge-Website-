OUR STORY

Keeping you connected, wherever you go.

Albert Ma, the founder of Go Charge, created a Canadian-based public charging solution designed for people who are constantly on the move. Go Charge was created after Albert noticed how often Canadians rely on their phones for daily tasks such as communication, navigation, and paymentsâ€”especially when they are away from home. Around 64% of Canadians use their smartphones at least once every hour, showing just how essential mobile devices are throughout the day.

Our Mission Statement is to keep Canadians connected by offering fast, affordable, and reliable charging wherever itâ€™s needed most. Our product is a public charging station that allows customers to rent or buy portable chargers instantly. Each charger is an 18W fast charger with retractable cables that support USB-C, Lightning, Mini USB, and Micro USB, so users never need to bring their own cable.

Customers can rent a charger for $2.35 every 45 minutes or purchase one for $55 for long-term use. Go Charge stations are placed in high-traffic public locations such as shopping malls, airports, train stations, schools, and tourist areas, beginning with the Toronto Eaton Centre and Scarborough Town Centre. With quick charging that can power phones up to 50% in just 25 minutes, up to 50 chargers per station, and pricing that is more affordable than competitors, Go Charge delivers the most convenient and reliable public charging solution made specifically for Canadians.

Sustainable Innovation

GoCharge promotes responsible consumption and production as a means of meeting the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 12. GoCharge has opted to offer reusable portable chargers on rent, rather than charging them using high-quality, disposable cables. This method helps in the reduction of electronic waste and minimizes environmental impact. GoCharge has contributed into reducing its carbon footprint by over 10,000 kg this year, thanks to the elimination of low quality charging devices.
