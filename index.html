Frequently Asked
Questions

How do I rent a charger?

Simply visit one of our kiosks, select the charger on the screen, pay through card or coins, and the power bank will release automatically.

Where can I return the power bank?

You can return it to any GoCharge station across the city. Just slide it into an empty slot, and your rental will end.

Do I need to bring my own cable?

No! Every GoCharge power bank comes with built-in cables for iPhone (Lightning), USB-C, Mini-Usb, and Micro-USB.

What happens to chargers that are never returned?

If you do not return a portable charger, it will stop charging and will be tracked by our staff to be retrieved. Please return your rentals and use responsibly!
